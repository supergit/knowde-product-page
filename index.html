Oligogeline™ PF

Oligogeline™ PF comes from a specific algal origin: chondrus crispus algae, a red seaweed living in deep water, submitted to extreme environmental conditions (e.g. ocean waves, UV exposure, dry conditions at low tide). Rich in carageenans, OLIGOGELINE™ PF has a "second skin" effect, forming a smooth and comfortable protective film on the skin's surface and reducing TEWL.

Technical product information

Product Background

OLIGOGELINE™ PF comes from a specific algal origin: chondrus crispus algae, a red seaweed living in deep water, submitted to extreme environmental conditions (UV exposure, dry conditions at low tide.

Mode of Action

OLIGOGELINE™ PF is a second skin ingredient that is forming a film on the skin's surface. This long-lasting action is due to bio-mimetic properties between skin proteins and carrageenans. They bind together, forming a film on skin's surface, like a second skin.

Product Benefits
  • Natural shield for pollution protection
  • 43% protection versus non treated area
  • 78% of microparticles quantity removed versus before rinsing
  • Second skin ingredient: TEWL -38% after 2 hours
  • Long lasting diffusion
